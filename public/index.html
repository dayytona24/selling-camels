The Ranch

The largest, most trusted camel ranch in Oklahoma.

We have spent years building a herd that buyers can stand behind. Every camel at HQ Encounters is raised on our land, handled daily, and halter-started well before it ever meets a new owner. That hands-on upbringing is why our animals arrive calm, sound, and ready to work or show.

Health comes first. Each camel is vet-checked, documented, and bred to standards we are willing to put our name on. Whether you want a Dromedary for riding, a Bactrian for cold-country work, or a Hybrid with the best of both, you are buying from people who know the individual animal, not a middleman guessing from a photo.

We sell direct, we answer honestly, and we stand behind every camel that leaves the ranch.
